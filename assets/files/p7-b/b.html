Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Camera Controls:

Use the arrow keys to pan the camera up (↑), down (↓), left (←), and right (→).

Use WASD keys to move the camera toward the aim point (W), away from the aim point (S), and to strafe left (A) and strafe right (D).

Mouse:

Click and drag the mouse within the viewports to rotate the stationary object.

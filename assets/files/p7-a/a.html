Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Bike: The bike is animated automatically and cannot be controlled manually.

Helicopter: You can interact with the helicopter in the following ways:

  • Rotate the helicopter: Click, hold, and drag your cursor within the canvas to rotate the helicopter.
  • Control the blades: Press ← (left arrow) and → (right arrow) to rotate the blades.
  • Control the rotors: Press ↑ (up arrow) and ↓ (down arrow) to rotate the rotors.
